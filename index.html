About

From box office to dashboard — data is data.

Hi, I'm Ebony Lopez, a recent graduate from the University of Washington Bothell with a Bachelor of Science in Data Visualization. I am passionate about using data to tell stories, solve problems, and create engaging experiences that help people understand information in meaningful ways.

Throughout my degree, I developed experience in data visualization, analytics, GIS, R, D3.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Excel. My work focuses on transforming complex datasets into accessible visual stories that help audiences explore trends, identify patterns, and make informed decisions.

In addition to my technical background, I have built extensive leadership and operational experience through AMC Theatres. I joined AMC in 2022 as a Crew Member and have progressed through multiple leadership roles, earning promotions each year. For the past two years, I have served as a manager supporting Human Resources, Food & Beverage operations, inventory management and ordering, booth operations, performance scheduling, hiring, onboarding, training, and employee development.

My operational experience spans every area of theatre operations, including concessions, ushering, box office, projection, food and beverage inventory management, ordering, and performance scheduling. I have assisted with projector troubleshooting, movie scheduling to help drive attendance and revenue, and theatre-wide initiatives focused on improving operational performance.

One of my favorite aspects of my work has been finding ways to apply data visualization principles outside of traditional analytics environments. I have built Excel-based tracking systems, performance dashboards, and employee engagement contests that transform operational data into interactive experiences. These projects have helped increase employee participation, improve performance metrics, and create stronger engagement with theatre goals.

My portfolio showcases both technical and operational projects, ranging from public data storytelling and interactive visualizations to workplace engagement systems designed to drive measurable business outcomes. I enjoy combining analytical thinking, visual design, and leadership to create solutions that are both effective and engaging.
